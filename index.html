Our Story

Born from Flame,
Shaped by Seasons

Stackly Restaurant began as a single wood-fired hearth in a converted warehouse. Chef Mara Voss believed that fire is not just a cooking method — it's a philosophy. Every dish honours the raw, the real, and the carefully sourced.

Today we work directly with twenty local farms, a heritage grain mill, and a rooftop apiary. The menu changes with the land, not with trends.
